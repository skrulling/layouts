Layout Library POC

Instructions:

Edit Mode ON: Click cells to select, drag to merge areas, drag rectangles to move, or hover near edges to resize.

Edit Mode OFF: View your layout with only the selected areas visible (no grid background).

Drag & Drop: Move rectangles around. Resize by hovering near edges until cursor changes, then drag.

Validation: Invalid operations (collisions or out-of-bounds) will be prevented or reverted.

Keyboard: Space to toggle mode, Escape to clear selection, Ctrl+E to export, Ctrl+R to reset.
